Geneva's murder mystery improv

Murderously entertaining long-form improv and immersive theater

"Visiting Artists & Community Collaborations" We occasionally host workshops and community events as part of our commitment to growing English-language improv in Geneva. These sessions are designed to be accessible, practical, and welcoming to improvisers from different backgrounds and experience levels. ((((Over the past years, members of The Murderet have been deeply committed to developing their own practice while supporting the growth of the local improv community. This has meant seeking out training locally and abroad, learning from experienced international performers and teachers, then inviting some of them to Geneva to share their knowledge with our community. In 2023, we hosted our first workshop in Geneva with an international artist from Baby Wants Candy and Shitzprobe (USA), and we’re excited to continue creating opportunities for learning, exchange, and play. These workshops are driven by a genuine love of improv and a belief that sharing knowledge strengthens everyone, on and off stage.))))
