About Us

We are The Murderet, Geneva's English-speaking troupe devoted to murder mystery and long-form improv. Since 2017, we've been crafting stories where comedy tangles with suspense, exploring long-form formats and immersive theater, always chasing the fun of discovering what might happen next!
