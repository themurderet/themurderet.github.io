Geneva's murder mystery improv

Murderously entertaining long-form improv and immersive theater

Our work

Beyond the stage, our work spans immersive theatre, which heavily relies on improv, as well as community-driven projects with the organization of workshops, a festival and a retreat. We have also been fortunate to collaborate with major institutions such as the Comédie de Genève (former and new) and the Museum of Art and History of Geneva through the Fête du Théâtre.
