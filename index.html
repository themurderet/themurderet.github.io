About Us

We are The Murderet, Geneva's English-speaking troupe devoted to murder mystery and long-form improv. Since 2017, we've been cooking up stories where comedy tangles with suspense, using a mix of playful creativity and storytelling. Along the way, we've also strayed into other formats, from fresh spins on long-form to immersive theatre, always chasing the fun of discovering what might happen next!
